LIFE AND WORK WITH MULTIPLE DOGS IS BETTER WITH TOOLS DESIGNED FOR IT

Inspired by the demands of professional dog walking, TinyHorse designed a suite of products to respond to the dynamics of managing multiple dogs on leash. Our system of leads and leashes connect in endless configurations so dog care professionals and dog lovers can customize their gear to their unique dog walking scenario.

Watch the video below to learn how to select and connect products from our collection to build your multi-dog walking system.

Explore the different categories mentioned in the video via our menu or just scroll past the video to find the products mentioned.

Select your Lead-All leashes

Use Lead-All leashes to attach dogs to connecting rings. Add as many Lead-All leashes to your cart as the number of dogs you walk at one time. Build your set from our collection below. Each option is a variation of the core design of our double-ended adjustable leashes. For example, Lite means it's made of classic nylon webbing and Pro means it's made of waterproof BioThane. And Extra leashes are longer than our standard-length leashes. Each model also has a lightweight mini option for smaller dogs available under the "width" menu item. One connecting ring comes complimentary with your set of leashes. Add additional rings to your order if you want to split your group into smaller clusters. We recommend this approach if there are significant differences in training and temperament within your group.
